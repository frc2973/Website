BEST Competitions

Each year, BEST releases a new theme for the annual competition. On Kickoff Day, the game is revealed and teams begin building the robot. Teams are only allowed to use the materials provided in the kit of parts. This year's game is called MADE2ORDER.

FIRST Competitions

Like BEST, FIRST releases a new theme each year for the annual competition. On Kickoff Day, the game is revealed and teams begin building the robot. Unlike BEST, teams are allowed to use whatever materials they have. This year's game is called CHARGED UP.

Typical Schedule

Wednesday

Arrival

On Wednesday, a small group from our team travels to the convention center to unload and set up the pit. There are no competition matches on this day.

Thursday

Practice

On Thursday, the team gathers for the first time around the field and prepares for practice rounds. This is the day when all of the minor (or major) problems are worked out.

Friday

Qualifying

Friday is the first day of qualifying. Practice rounds end and qualifying begins with each match counting towards a team's final ranking score.

Saturday

Playoffs

On Saturday, qualifying comes to an end and the top 8 teams choose their alliances for the knockout stages of the game. At the end, we have awards.
